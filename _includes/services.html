What we do

Learn

At Hope Incentives, we use applied evidence to improve people’s lives. We’re currently collecting data on how we can best reduce the college dropout rate in a scalable and repeatable way.

Analyze

Our team eats data for breakfast, spreadsheets for lunch and regressions for dinner. Hope Incentives seeks to marry the heart of a charity with the brain of a start-up, to figure out ways to help the world that are both compassionate and effective

Educate

The third phase of a Hope Incentives project is to turn over the steering wheel of a project to capable people who share our ideals and can implement the plan on a broader scale. To this end, we use our data to create toolkits and intend to transparently make everything we’re doing available and open-source.
